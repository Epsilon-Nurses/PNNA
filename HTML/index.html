Our Story

We were founded in 1989 by a small group of Filipino Nurses inspired by our first mentor Dr. Emperatriz Rabago. The original name “Filipino Nurses Association of Central California” was changed upon becoming an official chapter of the PNAA “Philippine Nurses Association of America” in 1991.

Together we are a strong force in meeting the professional needs of our members promoting the image of the Filipino Nurses in the Central Valley as essential members of a growing healthcare team through education, collaboration and participation.

PNACC continues it’s mission and is represented in local, state, national and nursing worldwide.
